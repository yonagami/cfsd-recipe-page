Make a timeless Daiquiri Hemingway

The legend says Ernesto invented this drink in a ginger joint in Havana. Like his books, the cocktail is a blend of flavors that melt in the palace of your mouth. you can do it in less than 5 minutes. Some say that the world record is 37 seconds. Most bartenders don't know how to do it. Follow the instructions below and take your time savouring your own Daiquiri.

Ingredients

  • 8 parts Bacardi rum
  • 3 parts lime juice
  • 1 part sour cherry jam
  • 1 part orange juice
  • 2 cubes of ice

Instructions

  1. Put the ice cubes into a shaker
  2. Add the rum
  3. Shake it (at least 5 seconds)
  4. Add the lime juice
  5. Shake it more (at least 5 seconds)
  6. Add the orange juice
  7. Shake again (at least 5 seconds)
  8. Add the cherry jam
  9. Shake for the last time (at least 10 seconds)
  10. Pour the daiquiri into your favorite whisky glass and enjoy it
